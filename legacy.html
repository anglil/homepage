Angli Liu is doing multifold research as a PhD student with the crowdlab and the netlab since Autumn 2013, after receiving a Bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Tsinghua University and a stint at Microsoft Research Asia. He is a believer in formally principled engineering and efforts to democratize and scale up technologies.

Research

  • With MSR: learning direct answer highlighting for Bing's query answer system (product)
  • With UW: algorithms that combine crowdsourcing and distant supervision to improve the performance relation extractors (Master's thesis, NAACL'16)
  • With MSR and UW: battery-free backscatter communication (HotMobile'15, SIGCOMM'14)
  • With MSR: non-Byzantine fault tolerant VM placement in Azure datacenters (product)
  • Earlier projects: direction-of-arrival estimation for anttena arrays, joint optimization for relay and modulation schemes in WiMAX, and energy efficient duty cycling for sensor networks
