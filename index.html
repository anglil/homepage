Angli Liu studies in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Washington since Autumn 2013, after receiving a Bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Tsinghua University and a stint at Microsoft Research Asia. He is a believer in formally principled engineering and efforts to democratize technologies. Right now he is working at the interplay of machine learning and natural language processing.
