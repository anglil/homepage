I study in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Washington since Autumn 2013, after receiving a Bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Tsinghua University and a stint at Microsoft Research Asia. I'm interested in understanding intelligence and creating intelligence. And I approach both ends with natural languages as the middle ground. I am a believer in efforts on technology democratization, which led me to have worked on ubiquitously accessible wireless networks.
